    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is Creators-Space?

    Creators-Space is an innovative online platform dedicated to tech learning, skill development, and empowering creators with industry-ready courses in programming, design, and more.

    How can I enroll in a course?

    To enroll, simply click the "Enroll Now" button on any course page, create an account if you haven't already, and follow the secure payment process. You'll gain immediate access to all course materials.

    What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept a variety of payment options including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and digital wallets like Paytm and Google Pay for a seamless checkout experience.

    Is there a refund policy?

    Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the course for any reason, contact our support team within 7 days of enrollment for a full refund.

    Do you provide certificates upon completion?

    Absolutely! Upon successfully completing a course, you'll receive a verifiable digital certificate that you can share on LinkedIn or your resume to showcase your skills.

    What is the mode of learning? Online or offline?

    All our courses are fully online, featuring high-quality recorded video lectures, interactive quizzes, live doubt-clearing sessions, and lifetime access to materials for flexible learning.
