🛡️ System Overview

This system ensures users watch videos completely before being eligible for course certificates. It prevents video skipping, tracks actual watch time, and validates legitimate learning progress.

🎯 Skip Prevention Features

🚫 Seek Prevention

Detects and limits video seeking/skipping beyond allowed thresholds

⏱️ Watch Time Tracking

Tracks actual continuous watch time vs total video duration

🎮 Keyboard Blocking

Prevents arrow keys and YouTube-style keyboard shortcuts

📊 Violation Monitoring

Counts skip violations and enforces maximum limits

🏆 Certificate Validation

Only grants certificates for properly watched content

👁️ Real-time Feedback

Shows warnings and progress indicators during video playback

🔧 Certificate Validation Criteria

  • Minimum Watch Time: 90% of actual video duration (not just position)
  • Skip Violations: Maximum 3 violations per lesson
  • Continuous Watching: Tracks real watch time vs. seeking
  • Session Monitoring: Detects legitimate vs. artificial progress
  • Course Completion: All lessons must be properly watched
